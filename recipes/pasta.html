Pasta

Description

Pasta is a type of food typically made from an unleavened dough of wheat flour mixed with water or eggs, and formed into sheets or other shapes, then cooked by boiling or baking.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Olive Oil
  • Cheese
  • Spices
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Capsicum

Steps

  1. Make the pasta dough from the flour
  2. Create Pasta from dough
  3. Boil some water and cook pasta
  4. Grate some cheese and add tomato sauce and ingredients
  5. Add spices and serve
